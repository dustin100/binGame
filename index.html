Waste Tip:

Paper coffee cups

Place Tim Hortons and other paper hot beverage cups in the garbage bin. The plastic lining and dyes on these paper cups make them difficult to recycle at this time. Non-black plastic lids and paper sleeves should be removed and placed in the Blue Bin.

For more tips and info visit the City of TO Waste Wizard site or download the TO Waste App.
